Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 25, 2026

Who we are

Make My Flight KC is a free flight departure calculator for Kansas City International Airport (MCI), built and operated by Yosef Silver (yosefsilver.com). You can reach us at departures@makemyflightkc.com.

What data we collect

We collect only what you voluntarily provide:

  • Email addresses submitted through the "Settle the Argument" form or the flight lookup waitlist form
  • We also collect anonymous usage data automatically via Google Analytics 4

How we use your data

  • Email addresses: used only to deliver the requested calculation result or to notify you when flight lookup is available
  • Analytics data: used to understand how the tool is being used and improve it

What we never do

  • We never sell your data
  • We never share your email with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We never store payment information (Ko-fi handles all donations independently)

Third-party services

  • Formspree (formspree.io): processes email form submissions. See Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google Analytics 4: collects anonymous usage data using cookies. You can opt out at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • Ko-fi (ko-fi.com): processes donations independently. See Ko-fi's privacy policy at ko-fi.com/privacy

Cookies

This site uses cookies for:

  • Google Analytics (anonymous usage tracking)
  • Storing your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Remembering that you dismissed the cookie notice

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. The calculator will still work without cookies.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your email at any time by emailing departures@makemyflightkc.com. We will remove it within 30 days.
