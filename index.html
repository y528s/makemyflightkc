Why Kansas City needed this

Make My Flight KC is a free departure calculator built specifically for Kansas City International Airport (MCI). It combines real drive times from KC neighborhoods, TSA security estimates, parking buffer times, and local conditions to tell you exactly when to leave for your flight.

How early should I arrive at MCI?

The new single terminal at MCI opened in 2023 and changed the math for every traveler. With an average gate walk of just 8 minutes and a compact security checkpoint, most domestic flyers can arrive 75-90 minutes before departure. TSA PreCheck holders often need just 60 minutes. But holidays, weather, and parking can shift that window significantly — that's what this calculator accounts for.

What factors affect my MCI leave time?

Drive time from your neighborhood with real traffic conditions, parking choice and availability, security lane type (Standard, PreCheck, or CLEAR), party size and mobility needs, weather delays, and whether you're flying domestic or international. We factor in all of these so you don't have to guess.

MCI parking tips

The terminal garage fills up on holiday weekends and Monday mornings. If the garage is full, the Economy Lot is your best backup — the blue shuttle runs every 20 minutes, 24/7. Off-site options like The Parking Spot offer a 15-minute buffer including their shuttle ride.
